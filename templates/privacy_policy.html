2. Important Definitions

Processing: Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, recording, organization, storage, adaptation, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure, or erasure.
Data Subject: Any identified or identifiable natural person whose personal data is processed by us.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR Article 6, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

4.1 Contractual Necessity (Article 6(1)(b))

Processing necessary for the performance of our Terms of Service contract, including account creation, service delivery, and core platform functionality.

4.2 Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f))

Processing necessary for our legitimate interests, including platform security, fraud prevention, service improvement, and direct marketing (where consent is not required).

4.3 Consent (Article 6(1)(a))

Processing based on your explicit consent for specific purposes such as marketing communications, optional features, or data sharing.

4.4 Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c))

Processing necessary to comply with legal obligations, including tax requirements, law enforcement requests, and regulatory compliance.

10. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject under GDPR, you have the following rights:

10.1 Right of Access (Article 15)

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you, including details about how it's processed.

10.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

10.3 Right to Erasure - "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17)

You can request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances, including when it's no longer necessary for the original purpose.

10.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

You can request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

10.5 Right to Object (Article 21)

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests, including direct marketing.

10.6 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)

You have rights regarding automated decision-making and profiling, though we do not currently engage in significant automated decision-making.

10.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

Exercising Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights, contact us at lifeeventmanager666@gmail.com. We will respond within one month and may request verification of your identity for security purposes.
